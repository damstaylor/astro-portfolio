Hostelworld, a travel booking app that is famous among backpackers, has a strong and shiny visual identity that reflects its young and laid-back spirit. Yet, the current version of the mobile app suffers from a few graphic design and functional irrelevances. Having used it many times as a backpacker, as part of the “Redesign an existing app” project I carried out at Ironhack Paris, I naturally decided to give it a fresh redesign!
